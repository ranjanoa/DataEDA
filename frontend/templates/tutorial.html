1 Uploading Your Data

Start by selecting your process data files (CSV or Excel) in the Data Files input. You can select multiple files at once. The app will automatically try to find a timestamp column to align your data.

2 Using a Legend

A LEGEND.csv file is optional but highly recommended. It allows the app to know the correct Units, Min, and Max ranges for your variables, making the plots look much better instantly.

3 Merging & Stats

Click Merge & Process Data to combine your files. Once processed, scroll down to see the Statistics Table. It shows "Stable Ranges" (where your process stays 50% of the time) and detects potential shutdowns automatically.

4 Advanced Visualizations

The Custom Plot Creator lets you build complex views:

  • Dual-Y: Compare two metrics with different units.
  • 3D Scatter: Visualize the relationship between three variables at once.
  • Correlation Heatmap: See which variables are linked.

5 AI Analysis 🤖

Enter your OpenAI or Gemini API key in the AI Assistant box. You can ask questions like "What caused the instability at 2 PM?" or "Summarize the main trends." The AI analyzes your data statistics to provide an expert opinion.
